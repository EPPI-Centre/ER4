This link allows Cochrane Authors to log into EPPI-Reviewer with their Cochrane Account details.
Within EPPI-Reviewer, Cochrane Authors are automatically licensed to:

  1. Work on Cochrane reviews (access control is controlled by review authorship state as defined in RevMan).
  2. Work on private (not shared) reviews.
  3. Work on "Prospective" Cochrane reviews (preliminary work, before it is formally given the Cochrane Review status). To gain access to this option please get in touch with EPPI-Support.
